this is 0x960, a bounded self-improvement environment for chess960 engine engineering.

the policy does not predict moves directly. it edits eval.py, runs matches, and is rewarded only when engine strength improves.

we measured +596.5 internal elo vs baseline and +221.1 elo over a stockfish-1320 anchor.

behavior quality also shifted from degenerate action loops to reliable write_file, run_match, finish execution.

the full stack combines teacher distillation, student sft, grpo refinement, and autonomous swarm-based engine search.
